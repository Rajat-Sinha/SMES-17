What is?

Society for Metallurgical and Material Engineering

Society for Metallurgical Engineering Students, NIT Jamshedpur has been continuously working for the development and upgradation of Metallurgical Engineering students of NIT Jamshedpur.

Ever since its inception, SMES has been working in harmony with the academia and industry for the common benefit of both.

It has been organising workshops, seminars, conferences and expert lectures by renowned metallurgists and scientists for the past two decades.
